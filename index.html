A local-first password vault. Generate, save, and analyze passwords without ever sending data off your device.

What it does

  • Password generator — basic, advanced, passphrase, and pronounceable modes.
  • Encrypted local vault — AES-encrypted storage with the key held in your OS secure enclave.
  • Biometric / PIN lock — optional re-auth on open and after short background timeouts.
  • Insights — strength distribution, reuse detection, and similarity warnings.
  • Portable export/import — encrypted backups can be restored on another device.
